SpiritFinder Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

Welcome to SpiritFinder! This Privacy Policy explains how Zuma Partners LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SpiritFinder mobile application ("App").

Quick Summary: We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We only collect information necessary to provide and improve SpiritFinder, and we never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use SpiritFinder, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Your preferences, interests, and spiritual journey details
  • Content: Any text, notes, or personal reflections you create within the app
  • Communications: Messages you send to us for support or feedback

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Our app automatically collects certain information to provide and improve our services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent in different sections, and app performance
  • Technical Data: App crashes, error logs, and performance metrics
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app functionality

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do not collect your precise location data
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other apps
  • We do not collect sensitive health information
  • We do not track your browsing history outside our app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver SpiritFinder features and functionality
  • Personalize Experience: Customize content and recommendations based on your preferences
  • Improve App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
  • Communicate: Send important updates, security notices, and support responses
  • Ensure Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate SpiritFinder, such as:

  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Payment processors (if applicable)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use secure cloud services with robust security measures

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Update: Correct or update your information through the app
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications (if any)
  • Control push notification settings through your device
  • Manage email preferences in your account settings

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide SpiritFinder services or as required by law. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

SpiritFinder is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Third-Party Services

SpiritFinder may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before using them.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification (if you have an account)
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of SpiritFinder after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
